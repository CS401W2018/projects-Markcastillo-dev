Understanding Image Formats

What kind of photos are good to encode using JPEG?

JPEG is best for photos with lots of colors, such as landscapes, portraits, or images. These are good to use also for website

What kind of photos are good to encode using GIF?

GIF is ideal for images with few colors, simple graphics, or small animations. It's best used for logos, icons, and images with flat, solid colors.

What is unique about the PNG format?

PNG offers lossless compression, which means no quality is lost during compression.]making it perfect for images that require sharp edges, such as logos and icons.

How do you resize your photo to the size it should be displayed?

Use photo editing software to resize the image you want to appear on your website. And helps to avoids unnecessary loading time.

Why should you resize photos using photo editing software instead of resizing it using CSS?

Resizing with photo editing software reduces the actual file size, improving performance by decreasing the amount of data that needs to be downloaded.
