Aquaman's story explores themes of identity, belonging, and the balance between two worlds, making him a complex and enduring figure in the superhero genre.

The Trident of Poseidon is a symbol of power.

Aquaman wields the powerful Trident of Poseidon, which grants him control over the oceans, the ability to manipulate water, and enhanced combat abilities. It's also a symbol of his authority as the King of Atlantis.

Atlantean Armor

Aquaman wears armor made from Atlantean materials that provides him protection both in the water and on land. The armor is lightweight and durable, built to withstand immense pressures and combat.

Aqualad (other called as "Storm")

Aquaman's pet and it is giant sea horse that Aquaman rides into battle. Storm is often shown as a loyal companion and a symbol of Aquaman's connection to the ocean and marine life.
