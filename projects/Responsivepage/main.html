Here's the Family Members

Uzumaki Naruto

Uzumaki Naruto dreams of becoming a Hokage. He is hardworking and became one of the best ninjas. He is friendly and perseveres through every training he has.

Hinata

Hinata Hyuga is a great mother who loves her children and has a very powerful eye technique. She's brave enough to stand up in critical moments for Naruto.

Himawari and Boruto

They are both Naruto's children with Uzumaki and Hyuga abilities. They are both talented and smart ninjas. They both learn the skills of their mother and Naruto.
